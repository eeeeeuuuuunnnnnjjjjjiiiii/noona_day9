A.ta stands for "I am the audience." A.ta is not just a brand that follows trends or pursues

fashion. We create clothing that captures individual tastes and curiosities within the context

of culture, like a film, allowing people to express themselves.


A.ta offers people the opportunity to become an audience and engage in meaningful

communication with the brand. Each customer personally connects with A.ta, becoming a

unique audience member. Our clothing is not just a product; it is an artistic piece that

evokes empathy and allows for self-expression.


The inspiration for the collection is the considered nonchalance of the way an artist

dresses, lives and works. Characterized by ease of wear, luxury of hand, and quality of production.

TWe aim to embody a culture like a film, going beyond fashion. Through our clothing, we

seek to create artistic connections with people and become a platform for sharing

individuality and stories.


Therefore, you are the audience.
